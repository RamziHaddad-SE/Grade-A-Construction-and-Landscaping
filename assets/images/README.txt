Drop real project photos here, then wire them into the pages (see ../../README.md).

Recommended shots to collect from Grade A:
  hero.jpg            a strong finished project (concrete + landscape together is ideal)
  project.jpg         a flagship completed job (why / about feature)
  crew.jpg            the team / truck / equipment (About page)
  grading.jpg         a leveled/graded lot or machine at work
  concrete.jpg        a fresh driveway / patio / flatwork
  pavers.jpg          a paver patio or walkway
  walls.jpg           a retaining wall or brick masonry
  landscaping.jpg     a finished landscape / sod / bed install
  ba-before.jpg       a raw/overgrown lot or tired yard BEFORE
  ba-after.jpg        the same property AFTER (graded, built, planted)

Tips: landscape orientation, ~1600px wide, compressed (JPG/WebP). Construction +
finished-landscape pairs sell the "two divisions, one crew" story best.
