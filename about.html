Our Story

Built in Tuscaloosa, brick by brick

Grade A Construction and Landscaping has been serving Tuscaloosa and West Alabama since 2005. We started with a simple idea: do quality work, treat people fairly, and stand behind every project — whether it's a graded lot, a poured driveway or a finished landscape.

Over the years we grew into a true two-division contractor. Our construction side handles grading, concrete, pavers, retaining walls and masonry. Our landscaping side handles design, installation, maintenance, irrigation and tree work. Most of our projects use both — which is exactly why having one accountable crew matters.

We're a proud, locally owned small business, and our name is on every job. That's why we call it Grade A.
